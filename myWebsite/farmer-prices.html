About this tool: Predictions use historical price trends and seasonal patterns from East African commodity market data (2022–2024), consistent with datasets published on Kaggle by WFP (World Food Programme) and FAO. Actual prices depend on local weather, supply shocks, transport costs, and policy changes. Treat predictions as a planning guide and verify with your local agricultural extension officer or cooperative before making major selling decisions.
How This Tool Works
📦

3 Years of Market Data

Uses monthly price records for 8 crops across 6 East African markets, covering 2022–2024 (36 data points per crop).

📐

Trend + Seasonal Analysis

Linear regression captures the long-term price trend. Seasonal indices account for harvest cycles, rainy seasons, and demand shifts.

💬

Plain-Language Advice

Results shown in simple terms with traffic-light colours — no farming degree or finance background needed to understand the output.
