    How Does This Tool Work?
    📋

    3 Years of Market Records

    We use monthly crop prices from 6 East African markets recorded between 2022 and 2024 — 36 months of real data for each crop.

    📅

    Seasonal Patterns

    Prices go up and down in the same pattern every year — high in the dry season, low after harvest. We use these patterns to predict what is likely to happen next.

    ✅

    Simple Advice — No Jargon

    We turn the numbers into plain advice: Sell Now, Wait, or No Rush. You do not need any finance or farming qualifications to use this tool.
